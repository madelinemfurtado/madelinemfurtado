INFINITY

This is a mini booklet that I designed surrounding the word "infinity." My project contraints limited my color palette to one color, and a select few typefaces. I communicated the meanings behind the word "infinity" through the various spreads of my booklet.

When going through the booklet, it is recognizable that there is an intended line that has blank space. I intentionally did this to communicate the meaning of infinity: there is no end. I italicized the word infinity for the title, and then continue to italicize the word "infinity" throughout my body copy in the spreads. This emphasizes the word and also ties into the overall graphics of my booklet.
